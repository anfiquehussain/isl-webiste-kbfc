Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium

The Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Kochi, Kerala, India. It is the home ground of Kerala Blasters FC and also hosts games of the Indian national football and cricket teams. The stadium has a seating capacity of 80,234 and is one of the largest stadiums in India.

Recent years (2021-present)
